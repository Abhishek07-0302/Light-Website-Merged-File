Timeline

  1. Jan 2017 Light Samvedna was established with the help of students from Govt. College of Engineering Aurangabad
  2. Feb 2018 Light Gaya was established with the help of students from Gaya College of Engineering
  3. March 2018Light Chennai was established with the help of students from IIITDM Kancheepuram
  4. Oct 2019Light Raipur was established with the help of students from NIT Raipur
  5. Nov 2019Light Pallakad was established with the help of students from IIT Pallakad, Kerala
  6. Nov 2019Light Jaipur was established with the help of students from MNIT Jaipur
  7. Feb 2020Light Wardha was established in Bajaj Institute of Technology, Wardha
  8. April 2020 Light Gwalior Sitholi was established with the help of students of ITM Group of Institutions, Gwalior
  9. May 2021 Light Gwalior Thatipur was established in May 2021. We are common people, we don't want to change the world, we want to improve it first.
  10. June 2021 LiGHT Bhubaneswar was established in June 2021 in online mode. The team is motivated to solve real world problems using innovative solutions.
  11. June 2021 LiGHT Sindri was established in June 2021 in online mode. Our team enthusiastically participated in the INTER CENTER COMPETITION and graciously organized its first event in the month of November.
  12. June 2021 Light Akola was established in June 2021 in a tough pandemic time period in India with a motivation to serve the community. For the last 6 months Light Akola has proved its capabilities by actively conducting events in online mode.

LIGHT

LIGHT is an initiative of one of India's largest student-run government registered NGO Gopali Youth Welfare Society, based in IIT Kharagpur. Gopali Youth Welfare Society aims at the overall socio-economic development of the underprivileged near the IIT Kharagpur campus.
