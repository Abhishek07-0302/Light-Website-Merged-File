LiGHT JAIPUR

Being a part of LiGHT we have a lot of things to learn and a platform to do something for society. LiGHT is an initiative of one of the largest student-run government registered NGOs based in IIT Kharagpur. It provides the perfect platform to perform our social responsibilities towards society. It is a fast-growing network and gives us guidelines to overcome major problems. Through LiGHT Jaipur, we hope to get exposure to the real-life problems of our country at the grass-root level. This platform helps in making people aware of problems surrounding them and the ways to solve these problems.   ... This platform ensures a life of dignity for adults by conducting relevant skill development programs. Being a part of LiGHT keeps us all connected to the problems and requirements of the society apart from our own needs. It is definitely a medium for enhancement of our own social skills and problem tackling ability.Issues like Quality education is something that has always demanded a reform and through this platform, we can bring a change in it. This interactive platform provides social, educational, and economic empowerment to underprivileged sections of the society.

LiGHT

LiGHT is an initiative of one of India's largest student-run government registered NGO Gopali Youth Welfare Society, based in IIT Kharagpur. Gopali Youth Welfare Society aims at the overall socio-economic development of the underprivileged near the IIT Kharagpur campus.
