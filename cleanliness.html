OVERVIEW OF THE DRIVE

Cleanliness Drive was the first nationwide event conducted by 9 LiGHT Centers. It was conducted on 2nd October 2019 on the occasion of the birth anniversary of The Father of the Nation: Mahatma Gandhi and the launch of Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan. Cleanliness was the annual theme of LiGHT of the session 2019 - 2020 under which we conducted several cleanliness campaigns across the country.

Aim of the Drive

  • It aimed to instill the importance of cleanliness among the youth.
  • Facilitate the important facts and create awareness about cleanliness.
  • It aimed to mark the launch of the Swachh Bharat Mission from LiGHT.
